example, Affirm seeks to cut credit card companies out of the online shopping process by offering a way for consumers to secure immediate, loans for purchases. Similarly, Better Mortgage seeks to streamline the home mortgage process (and obviate traditional mortgage brokers) with a digital-only offering that can reward users with a verified pre-approval letter within 24 hours of applying.
